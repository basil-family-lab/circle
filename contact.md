# Connect with Michael Basil

![Michael Basil](/images/profile.png)

Michael Basil is a coach, facilitator, and guide for leaders navigating complexity. He helps leaders and teams act with clarity and steadiness when situations are ambiguous, priorities compete, and outcomes matter. His work draws on practical Zen Leadership tools and embodied disciplines—including his ongoing Aikido practice—to foster sound judgment, aligned action, and forward momentum.

Before guiding leaders through change, Michael spent years in technology and organizational transformation roles, working across universities and multinational organizations. He observed that the biggest obstacles to progress are rarely technical—they’re relational and systemic. That insight shapes how he works with leaders and teams using The Circle: small, repeatable spaces for shared understanding, alignment, and meaningful action.

Whether you’re curious about starting a Circle, exploring conscious conversation, or seeking coaching for yourself or your team, Michael welcomes a conversation.
