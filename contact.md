# Connect with Michael Basil

![Michael Basil](/images/profile.png)

Michael Basil is a coach and facilitator who helps leaders navigate complex change with clarity and steadiness.

He draws on years of experience in technology, organizational transformation, and executive coaching, as well as practical Zen Leadership and embodied disciplines—including ongoing Aikido practice—to guide leaders and teams through ambiguity and competing priorities.

Michael works with leaders and teams using **The Circle**: small, repeatable spaces where people make sense of change together, align around what matters, and move into meaningful action.

Whether you’re curious about starting a Circle, exploring conscious conversation, or seeking coaching for yourself or your team, the first step is to reach out and connect.
